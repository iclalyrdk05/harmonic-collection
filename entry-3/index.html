Cocktail Culture

Origins and Evolution

Cocktails first appeared in 1806 as a mix of spirits, sugar, water, and bitters. Classics like the Manhattan and Martini appeared post-Prohibition.

Modern Trends

The craft cocktail movement focuses on quality ingredients and innovative techniques, shaping modern mixology trends.

Global Influence

Cocktail culture has spread worldwide, with regions like Japan adding unique twists. Bars serve as social hubs globally.
