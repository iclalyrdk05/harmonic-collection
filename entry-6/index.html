Şalgam Culture - Taste the Tradition of Adana

What is Şalgam?

Şalgam is a traditional drink made from salted, fermented black carrot and turnip juice. It comes in two varieties: acılı (spicy) and acısız (non-spicy).

Health Benefits

  • Rich in Vitamin B
  • Promotes digestion
  • Supports stomach and liver health
  • Contains calcium, potassium, and iron

When to Drink Şalgam

Enjoy Şalgam during meals, festive gatherings, after a night out, or just for its health benefits!
